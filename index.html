About the Foundation

Ray of Hope Foundation is a NOT FOR PROFIT SOCIAL STARTUP whose sole motive is to be a Supporting Framework for Institutes that work for Social Causes like Orphanages,Old-Age Homes,Make-shift Schools etc. We work with such Social Organizations, which are basically lacking all the essential funds to operate.
The Funds raised by the Ray Of Hope Foundation goes towards the Development and Sustainance of various Social Organizations.
